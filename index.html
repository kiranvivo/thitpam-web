About Us

Main objective of ‘Thitpam’
‘Create awareness of one’s own self’
by knowing one’s own traits, feelings and behavior through standardized tools.

‘Thitpam’ functions for transforming a person to
– an individual with strong mind capable of handling any situation.

Research and practical experience in all organisations have indicated clearly
the more the people know about themselves (likes, dislikes, strengths, improvements,emotional stability, social boldness and so on), the more productive, successful and happy, they are, in their career.

We are unaware of the power lying within us and search it outside.
‘Thitpam’ helps in
understanding and capturing the ‘Power within us’.
