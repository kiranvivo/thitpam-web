About Us

The main objective of ‘Thitpam’ is to create an awareness of one’s own self by knowing one’s own traits, feelings and behavior through standardized tools. ‘Thitpam’ functions with the main motto of creating an individual – a solid, certain and firm one, by making him/her understand himself/herself better.

Research and practical experience in both small and large organizations have indicated strongly that more the people know about themselves (regarding their likes, dislikes, strengths, weaknesses, interests, motives, etc) with relation to a specific job, career or profession, the more productive and successful they can be in their endeavours, along with being completely happy at the same time. Therefore, learning about the self is most important for increasing the odds of being successful.

Most of the time, we are unaware of the power lying within us and search for it outside. ‘thitpam’, standing by its name, helps us in understanding and capturing this “Power within us”.
